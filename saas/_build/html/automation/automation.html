3. AutomationÂ¶

  • 3.1. Pre-requisites

enStratus automation encompasses many facets of infrastructure mangement and orchestration.

  1. Automated backups. enStratus will perform backup/maintenance of the application and hardware infrastructure according to the specification of the deployment. Backups include both snapshots and service-level backups.
  2. Scalability. enStratus will scale the servers to closely match infrastructure with demand.
  3. Resiliency. Cloud servers by their nature are ephemeral, configuring a deployment will allow applications to survive the unexpected termination of a node.
